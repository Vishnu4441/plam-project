Coconut coir Pith


Coconut coir Pith / Coir dust is an organic natural product derived from coconut husk and its widely used for farming.

The coco peat is dried and processed to produce different items namely Blocks , Briquettes, Tablets etc. It is an excellent growing medium for hydroponics or container plant growing.


Hydroponics


Hydroponics is a type of hydro culture, basically involves growing healthy plants without the use of a traditional soil medium. Hydroponic plants may not have any solid material beneath them; rather, their roots often simply hang in water with a rich mix of dissolved nutrients in it. The primary advantage of hydroponics is the savings from minimal labor costs, as it is usually carried out in confined areas and mechanical irrigation and fertilization is performed.The nutrients in hydroponics can be from fish waste, duck manure, or normal nutrient.

Coco Peat ?


Coir Pith is the process of taking out of coir fiber from husk.

Coir fiber pith or coir dust can hold large quantities of water, just like a sponge.

Coco peat is the best alternative for peat moss as it create environmental hazard, since it cause land mining and renewability.

Coco peat has a unique ability to withstand high compaction force without leaving its beneficial structure.

It is known for its excellent soil conditioning properties and is therefore being extensively used as a soil-less medium for Horti, Flori & Agricultural purposes.

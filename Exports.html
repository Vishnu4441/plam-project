We ship worldwide

We export our product from one of the largest sea port in India , Tuticorin. Our product is usually shipped in the form of compressed bales, briquettes, slabs or discs. The end user usually expands and aerates the compressed coco peat by the addition of water.

We have qualified and experienced personnel who are capable to handle local/international shipment swiftly and smartly. They are capable to effectively deliver the goods to our valuable customers doorstep. This requires professionals following such reporting protocols to deal with current export formalities of the origin and the destination countries. As we finish the necessary formalities at both ends, our customers will have plenty of time focusing their energies on endorsing their market.

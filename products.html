Coco Chips

Coco coir husk chips, an organic and natural mulch made of coconut shells (husk) give them a highly sustainable alternative to other mulch options. The durable husk-chips of coconut fiber can withstand inclement weather and break down at a more measured and even rate than other bark chips.

Coco chip are added to the soil or mixed with Coco Peat to regulate the fermentation of the growing medium and maintain the ideal ratio of air to moisture content for the plant roots. All small fibers and dust are removed to ensure superior quality substrate.

It is basically great for all plants that combines a balance pH level , low natural salt content (Low EC) and minimize odar that make these coconut chip perfect for nurturing of all plants.

Coco chip are excellent choice for orchids, bromeliads, kalanchoe and any plants that require aeration.

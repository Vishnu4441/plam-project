Why we're here

PALM PEATS supplies 100% natural and organic coir and cocopeat products. It specialise in the provision of environment friendly , soil free products used for horticultural and agriculture applications.

In a world full of belch out pollution , here is a range of natural products that not only complement the user but also brought healthier environment. This is an initiative to set up not only a business activity but also a step to bring about a change for the better.

PALM PEATS also specialise in processing and exporting of eco friendly coir products for sustainable agricultural and industrial usage.

We are always searching for new and better solutions to achieve more practical experience and to be able to test and control the different products of soil substrate with excellent water holding capacity.
